Salt Lake County Reviews

I have a genuine passion for dining out in Salt Lake City. The city offers a vibrant array of activities, accompanied by a diverse culinary scene. During the summer months, one of my absolute favorite spots is Sweet Lake Biscuits and Limeade. This charming eatery provides a refreshing brunch experience, boasting a delightful selection of house specials. Every dish is crafted with locally sourced ingredients, and the limeade is freshly squeezed in-house, creating a crisp and invigorating taste that truly embodies a fresh culinary adventure.

The Blue Iguana stands out as the perfect choice for any evening event in the city. Nestled in a convenient location, this charming Mexican restaurant is an ideal spot for a casual date with your loved one. I highly recommend savoring the delightful flavors of the shrimp tacos and the must-try chimichanga – a surefire way to enhance your dining experience.

Fleming's Steakhouse stands as a distinguished dining destination, reserved for those special occasions and celebratory holidays. An ideal setting for family gatherings, the generous portion sizes not only justify the prices but elevate the dining experience. Personally, my favorites include the exquisite tomahawk steak and the wedge salad.

Utah County Reviews

Station 22 holds a special place in my heart. My boyfriend and I made a memorable stop for their famous chicken and waffles before our high school graduation. The vintage ambiance of the building adds to the charm, and if you're planning a visit on the weekends, arriving early is a must as things tend to get busy in no time.

The Purple Turtle is your go-to spot for satisfying those late-night cravings with its swift and laid-back burger haven. Indulge in the finest milkshakes, burgers, and fries that perfectly capture the essence of American originality.

Bam Bam's BBQ, nestled in the heart of Provo, proudly stands as a family-owned gem. The visionary owner, a remarkable individual, not only champions fellow entrepreneurs but is also a staunch supporter of the local college community. While the menu exudes simplicity, the array of sauces and flavors offered is truly unparalleled. A visit to Bam Bam's is a culinary adventure that is a must-try, making it an ideal destination for delightful family outings.
